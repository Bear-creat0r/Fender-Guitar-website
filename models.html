Stratocaster

The Stratocaster (Strat) is Fender's most iconic electric guitar — known for its contoured body, three single-coil pickups and versatile tone used across genres.

Telecaster

The Telecaster (Tele) is Fender's classic two-pickup solid-body guitar — celebrated for its bright, cutting tone and simple, rugged design.

Precision Bass

Introduced in 1951, the Precision Bass (P-Bass) revolutionized low-end in popular music with a solid body and a focused, punchy tone.

Jazz Bass

The Jazz Bass (J-Bass) offers a slimmer neck and a brighter, more articulate voice compared to the P-Bass — favored by many groove and session players.

Jazzmaster

The Jazzmaster features an offset waist body, unique floating tremolo and a warm, mellow sound popular with surf, indie and alternative players.

Jaguar

The Jaguar has a shorter-scale neck and distinctive switching options, producing a bright, chimey tone that works well for cutting through mixes.

Quick specs

Common features: Bolt-on maple neck, alder or ash bodies, single-coil or split pickups, classic Fender tremolo options.
